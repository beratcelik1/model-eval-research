# Health Longevity Sources

References used across the health longevity knowledge base.

## protocol-design.md

1. Peter Attia, M.D. (2023). "Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity." Four Horsemen framework, five pillars.

2. Bryan Johnson. Blueprint protocol.
   - https://protocol.bryanjohnson.com/

3. PREDIMED trial. 7,447 participants, median 4.8 years. Mediterranean diet + EVOO reduced major cardiovascular events by 30%.
   - https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1800389

4. Mandsager et al. (2018). VO2 max and mortality. Cleveland Clinic, 122,000+ patients.
   - https://peterattiamd.com/how-does-vo2-max-correlate-with-longevity/

5. 2025 Sports Medicine narrative review. Zone 2 as optimal for mitochondrial capacity questioned.

6. 2022 meta-analysis. Resistance training and bone density (hip BMD +0.64%, spine BMD +0.62%).

7. 2025 network meta-analysis. High-intensity resistance exercise superior for bone density.

8. 2025 review of TRE clinical trials (2015-2025). 3-5% body weight reduction, HbA1c improvement 0.3-0.5%.

9. 2024 Nature study. 960 genetically diverse mice. Genetics > dietary restriction for lifespan.

## supplement-research.md

10. Yoshino, S. et al. (2021). "Nicotinamide mononucleotide increases muscle insulin sensitivity in prediabetic women." Science. 25 women, 250mg/day.
    - https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33888596/

11. 2024 NMN systematic review and meta-analysis. Significant NAD elevation, no significant clinical outcomes.

12. 2025 NMN meta-analysis. No significant effects on skeletal muscle index, grip strength, gait speed.

13. February 2026 NMN RCT. 1,200mg/day reduced inflammatory signals but abolished 171% increase in muscle mitochondria from exercise.
    - https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/15502783.2026.2632284

14. June 2025 NR trial in Werner syndrome. NR boosted NAD+ and improved cardiovascular markers and skin ulcers.

15. FDA NMN ruling (September 29, 2025). NMN confirmed lawful for dietary supplements.
    - https://www.npanational.org/news/fda-reinstates-nmn-as-dietary-supplement-after-npa-lawsuit/

16. Berberine meta-analysis. 46 clinical studies. Equally effective as metformin.
    - https://www.scirp.org/journal/paperinformation?paperid=113351

17. Berberine vs metformin RCT. Prediabetic patients, berberine reduced FPG by 12.6 mg/dL vs metformin 10.8 mg/dL.
    - https://www.ijbcp.com/index.php/ijbcp/article/view/6037

18. VITAL trial. 25,871 participants, 5.3 years. 28% heart attack reduction, 50% fatal heart attack reduction.
    - https://www.vitalstudy.org/findings.html

19. REDUCE-IT trial. 4g icosapent ethyl (EPA). 25% relative risk reduction in cardiovascular events.
    - https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1812792

20. Creatine cognitive meta-analysis (2024). 16 RCTs, 492 participants. Improved memory, attention, processing speed.
    - https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11275561/

21. EFSA creatine cognition evaluation (2024). Evidence "suggestive but not yet conclusive."
    - https://efsa.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.2903/j.efsa.2024.9100

22. Chandrasekhar, K. et al. (2012). Ashwagandha RCT (KSM-66). 64 subjects, 600mg/day, 60 days. Cortisol reduced 27.9%.
    - https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3573577/

23. 2025 magnesium bisglycinate RCT. 155 adults, 250mg elemental Mg. ISI improved -3.9 vs -2.3 placebo.
    - https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12412596/

24. Endocrine Society Vitamin D Guidelines (2024). No longer endorses specific optimal 25(OH)D target.
    - https://www.endocrine.org/clinical-practice-guidelines/vitamin-d-for-prevention-of-disease
