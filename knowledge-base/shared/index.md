# Shared Knowledge Base

Cross-domain research and methodology that applies to all three verticals (investment, marketing, health).

## Pages

- [Eval Methodology](eval-methodology.md) - What makes a good eval, scoring systems, LLM-as-judge patterns, how evals become training signals
- [Model Comparisons](model-comparisons.md) - How to compare models fairly, existing frameworks, public data on Grok vs GPT-4 vs Claude vs Gemini
- [Existing Benchmarks](existing-benchmarks.md) - What benchmarks exist per domain, what they measure, and the critical gap between knowledge and usefulness

## How to Use This Wiki

Each page is a living document. When new research is found or conclusions change, update the relevant page and add an entry to [log.md](log.md).

Pages should be practical, not encyclopedic. Write what matters for building evals, not what sounds impressive in a paper.
